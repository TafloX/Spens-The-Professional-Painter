About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate painter specializing in residential and commercial properties in the Cape Winelands District, Paarl, Wellington, Franchhoek, Klein Drakenstein, Groot Drakenstein, Boland, Klapmuts, Malmesbury areas and surrounding areas. Customer satisfaction is of uttermost importance in the survival of my business. Having been in the construction industry for over 15 years with close to 10 years strictly in painting and waterproofing, I have built strong business partnerships with renowned artisans in tiling, electricity, plumbing, carpentry, bricklaying, plastering, etc. I have flexible pricing plans depending on site assessment. When painting outside, if need be, I highly recommend cleaning the gutters and the High pressure wash to expose all the problematic areas. Please note that in some residential setups, we might not be allowed to use the High pressure wash on asbestos. All the roof leaks will be waterproofed before any roof paintwork commences. I am more than happy to discuss with you the best recommendations to bring your property back to life at an affordable and flexible price. For your tailored home improvement solutions, please submit your information through the contact form on this page.
